About Pyrogenic Engineerings (I) Pvt Ltd

Our Story

Pyrogenic Engineerings (I) Pvt Ltd was established with a dream and determination of offering to customers the latest in Locking, Latching technology and related Control Panel & Machinery Components. We have been associated with major OEM's, Rack Fabricators and Panel Builders all over India with dealers spread over.

Our Product range Include World Class Industrial Panel Locks and Keys, Canopy Locks & Hinges, 3 & 4 Point Locks, Castle Interlocks, Fan Filters, Door Handles and Panel Accessories. Our Products are now being exported to the Middle East, Far East and Africa.
